The Superior Carpet & Upholstery Care Story

In 2005 Superior Carpet & Upholstery Care opened in the Des Moines, IA area with one van and one technician. Since then we have grown to a six van, eleven technician operation with one goal in mind:

"We strive to offer the finest textile and tile cleaning in Des Moines, IA and surrounding areas."

To do this our technicians train with our master certified technician. They then, when ready, attend courses to acquire certifications of their own. This allows us to offer consistant high quality services and products. You can trust Superior Carpet & Upholstery Care wether it's cleaning carpets, upholstery, synthetic or natural fiber area rugs, tile & grout, restretching or repairing carpets or mitigating water damage in your home that there are highly trained, certifed and motivated technicians on the job giving you the best service possible.

Starting with the high powered, truck mounted Prochem Apex GTX heater and vaccuum Superior Carpet & Upholstery Care uses some of the finest, top of the line equipment and tools in the industry. Accompany them with cleaning products that are tough on dirt and stains, but easy on the environment and you get a powerful combination that gives you a superior clean and protection for your carpet, upholstery and tile

Here at Superior Carpet & Upholstery Care we pride ourselves on the level of quality we provide to our customers.
